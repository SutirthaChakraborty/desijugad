Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This is a short, plain-English privacy policy because we have very little to tell you. The summary: we don't receive your files, we don't collect personal data, and we don't track individual users.

Your files

Every conversion tool on Desi Jugad runs entirely in your web browser. When you drop a file onto a converter, that file stays on your device. It is loaded into your browser's memory, processed there, and written back to your disk as the output. We never receive your files, we never store them, and we never have access to them.

You can verify this yourself by opening your browser's Network tab while converting — no upload request will appear.

Information we collect

We collect only basic, anonymous usage analytics to understand which tools are used and where problems occur. Specifically:

  • Page view counts — which pages are visited, aggregated across all users.
  • Approximate country — derived from your IP address at the time of the request. We don't store your IP address.
  • Browser and device type — so we know whether to prioritize mobile bug fixes or desktop ones.
  • Referring website — how you found us.

This data cannot identify you as an individual. We do not use user-level cookies, fingerprinting, or any tracking technology that follows you across sessions.

Advertising

We may display advertisements from third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense) to keep the service free. When present, these networks may use their own cookies and tracking per their respective privacy policies. Most modern browsers let you block these cookies via a single setting; we fully support that choice.

Third-party libraries

Converters load open-source JavaScript libraries from public CDNs (jsDelivr, Cloudflare CDN). The CDN provider may log the fact that a request was made. They do not see the files you convert — only the request for the library itself.

Cookies

We do not use first-party cookies for tracking or analytics. We may set a minimal functional cookie to remember your preferences (like your preferred conversion options) if you change defaults. These cookies are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere.

Data retention

Aggregate analytics data is retained for up to 26 months, then deleted. Individual-level data is never retained because it is never collected.

Your rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is generally nothing for us to correct, export, or delete on your behalf. If you believe we have collected personal data about you inadvertently, contact us at the email on our contact page and we'll investigate.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, post a notice on the homepage.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the contact page.
