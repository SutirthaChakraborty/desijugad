Bangladesh Income Tax Calculator 2026 — Salary Tax NBR Guide

Published April 28, 2026 · 7 min read

Bangladesh's income tax is administered by the National Board of Revenue (NBR). The tax year runs from July 1 to June 30. Salaried employees pay tax through a payroll withholding system. Here's how Bangladesh income tax works for FY 2025-26.

Bangladesh Income Tax Slabs FY 2025-26

For Male Taxpayers

  • 0% — First BDT 3,50,000
  • 5% — Next BDT 1,00,000
  • 10% — Next BDT 4,00,000
  • 15% — Next BDT 5,00,000
  • 20% — Next BDT 5,00,000
  • 25% — Above BDT 20,00,000

For Female Taxpayers & Senior Citizens (65+)

Tax-free threshold is BDT 4,00,000 (women) or BDT 4,75,000 (persons with disabilities). Same slab rates apply above the threshold.

Minimum Tax Liability

Taxpayers residing in major cities must pay a minimum tax even if their taxable income is low:

  • Dhaka & Chittagong: BDT 5,000 minimum tax
  • Other city corporations: BDT 4,000 minimum tax
  • Other municipalities: BDT 3,000 minimum tax
  • Other areas: BDT 2,000 minimum tax

Investment Tax Rebate

Bangladesh offers a tax rebate of 15% on investments up to 25% of total income (capped at BDT 1 crore). Qualifying investments include:

  • Life insurance premiums
  • Provident fund contributions
  • Government savings certificates (Sanchayapatra)
  • Deposits with post offices, scheduled banks
  • Shares in listed companies, mutual funds
  • Provident fund, gratuity fund

How to File Income Tax Return in Bangladesh

  1. Register for e-TIN (Tax Identification Number) on NBR portal (nbr.gov.bd)
  2. File return online (e-Return) by November 30 each year
  3. Attach investment proof documents
  4. Pay any tax due via bank challan

Key Deadlines

  • Tax return deadline: November 30 (for individual taxpayers)
  • Tax Day: November 30 (National Tax Day — special events at tax offices)
  • Companies: Different deadlines based on fiscal year end

Bangladesh VAT

Bangladesh VAT (Mushak) is 15% on most goods and services. Essential items like basic food staples, agricultural inputs, and export goods are exempt. Use the VAT calculator and enter 15% for Bangladesh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is salary income taxed monthly or annually in Bangladesh?

Tax is deducted monthly by employers (TDS - Tax Deducted at Source) from salary payments. At year end, the employer issues a tax certificate. A return must still be filed annually even if all tax was withheld.

What is the e-TIN requirement for bank accounts?

Banks require e-TIN for accounts with transactions above BDT 5 lakh per year. e-TIN is also required for property registration, trade licenses, and import/export operations.
