What Is a Favicon?

A favicon (favorite icon) is the small image that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and address bars. It's the visual identity of your website.

Why Use a Favicon?

  • Brand recognition in browser tabs
  • Professional website appearance
  • Improves user experience
  • Appears in bookmarks and history
  • Required by modern web standards

Favicon Formats

Format Use Case Size
ICO Traditional favicon 16×16 to 256×256px
PNG Modern browsers 32×32, 64×64, 512×512px
Apple Touch iOS devices 180×180px
