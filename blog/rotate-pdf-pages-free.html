How to rotate PDF pages online free — fix scanned documents (2026)

6 min read·April 2026

You scan a document and some pages come out sideways or upside down. You receive a PDF where half the pages are landscape when they should be portrait. You need to rotate just one page of a multi-page document. Our browser-based PDF rotation tool fixes all of these — free, private, no upload required.

Why PDFs end up with wrong page orientation

Several common scenarios produce PDFs with rotated pages:

  • Flatbed scanner in wrong direction: Placing a document sideways on a scanner produces landscape pages in a portrait document.
  • Mobile scanning apps: Google PhotoScan, CamScanner, and similar apps sometimes misdetect orientation, especially for text-heavy pages with no clear top/bottom.
  • Mixed orientation documents: A report with some portrait pages and some landscape charts — the landscape pages may appear rotated when the PDF is opened in certain viewers.
  • PDF metadata corruption: Some PDFs have correct rotation metadata that certain viewers ignore, making pages appear rotated.
  • Page rotation metadata: PDF pages store a rotation value (0°, 90°, 180°, 270°) as metadata. Some tools set this incorrectly.

How PDF rotation works technically

PDF pages have a /Rotate entry in their page dictionary that specifies the clockwise rotation angle to apply when displaying the page. Valid values are 0, 90, 180, and 270. When you "rotate a PDF page", you're updating this metadata value — the actual page content doesn't change.

This means rotating a PDF is completely lossless: no re-encoding, no quality loss, no size increase. The text, images, and vector graphics remain completely intact — only the display orientation changes.

How to rotate PDF pages in your browser

  1. Open the PDF Rotate tool.
  2. Drop your PDF file.
  3. The tool shows thumbnail previews of all pages.
  4. Click individual pages to select them, or use "Select All".
  5. Click "Rotate 90° Clockwise", "Rotate 90° Counter-clockwise", or "Rotate 180°".
  6. Preview updates instantly — click again to rotate further.
  7. Click Save PDF to download the corrected file.

Uses pdf-lib in your browser. No files leave your device.

Rotate all pages vs specific pages

Our tool supports both:

  • All pages: Click "Select All" → choose rotation direction → Save. Use this when a scanner placed every page sideways.
  • Specific pages: Click individual page thumbnails to toggle selection → rotate only selected. Use this when a mixed document has a few problem pages.
  • Page ranges: Hold Shift to select a range of pages by clicking the first and last.

Common use cases

  • Fixed scanned bank statements: Mobile banking PDFs that came out rotated 90°.
  • Government form submissions: Income tax, passport, IRCTC documents with upside-down pages.
  • Academic papers with landscape figures: Rotating the landscape figure pages to display correctly in a portrait document.
  • Real estate documents: Floor plans scanned in landscape that appear sideways in the full document.
