What Is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined way to invest in mutual funds by contributing a fixed amount regularly (monthly, quarterly, etc.).

Why Use a SIP Calculator?

  • Calculate expected returns from your SIP investment
  • Plan your retirement savings
  • Compare different return scenarios
  • Track maturity amount and gain
  • Calculate XIRR (actual returns)
