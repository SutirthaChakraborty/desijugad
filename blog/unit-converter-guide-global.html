Unit Converter Guide — Miles, Kg, Celsius, Stone & Global Units

Published April 28, 2026 · 10 min read

Different countries use different measurement systems — and it causes endless confusion. The USA uses miles and Fahrenheit. The UK uses miles on roads but kg in supermarkets and stones for body weight. Europe uses metric for everything. India uses metric plus unique traditional units for land and gold. This guide covers all the common conversions you'll ever need.

Length Conversions — US, UK, Metric

  • 1 mile = 1.609 km (USA, UK roads use miles)
  • 1 km = 0.621 miles
  • 1 foot = 0.305 meters (USA/UK use feet for height)
  • 1 meter = 3.281 feet
  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm
  • 1 yard = 0.914 meters
  • 1 nautical mile = 1.852 km (aviation, shipping)

Weight Conversions — Pounds, Stones, Kilograms

  • 1 kg = 2.205 pounds (lbs) — used in USA, Nigeria, and most countries for body weight labeling
  • 1 pound = 0.454 kg
  • 1 stone = 14 pounds = 6.35 kg — UK and Ireland use stones for body weight (e.g., "I weigh 11 stone")
  • 1 US ton = 2,000 lbs = 907 kg
  • 1 metric ton = 1,000 kg
  • 1 ounce = 28.35 grams

Gold Weight Units (India, Pakistan, Middle East)

  • 1 tola = 11.664 grams — standard gold measurement in India/Pakistan
  • 1 troy ounce = 31.103 grams — international gold standard
  • 1 carat (diamond) = 0.2 grams = 200 mg

Temperature Conversions — Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin

  • C to F: F = (C × 9/5) + 32
  • F to C: C = (F - 32) × 5/9
  • C to K: K = C + 273.15

Key reference points:

  • 0°C = 32°F (water freezes)
  • 100°C = 212°F (water boils)
  • 37°C = 98.6°F (body temperature)
  • -40°C = -40°F (the only point where both scales are equal)
  • 20°C = 68°F (comfortable room temperature)
  • 30°C = 86°F (hot summer day)

Volume Conversions — Gallons, Liters, Cups

  • 1 US gallon = 3.785 liters
  • 1 UK/Imperial gallon = 4.546 liters
  • 1 US cup = 236.6 ml
  • 1 US fluid ounce = 29.57 ml
  • 1 US tablespoon = 14.79 ml
  • 1 liter = 1.76 UK pints = 2.11 US pints

Area Conversions — Global Land Measurement Units

  • 1 acre = 4,047 m² = 0.405 hectares (USA, UK, Africa, Caribbean)
  • 1 hectare = 10,000 m² = 2.471 acres (EU, international standard)
  • 1 square mile = 640 acres = 2.59 km² (USA)
  • 1 bigha (UP) ≈ 2,500 m² = 0.25 hectares (North India — varies by state)
  • 1 marla = 25.29 m² (Pakistan/Punjab)
  • 1 kanal = 505 m² = 20 marla (Pakistan)
  • 1 gunta = 101.17 m² (Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh)
  • 1 cent = 40.47 m² (South India, Sri Lanka)

Fuel Efficiency — MPG, L/100km, km/L

  • USA: MPG (miles per gallon). Higher = more efficient.
  • Europe: L/100km (liters per 100km). Lower = more efficient.
  • India: km/L (kilometers per litre). Higher = more efficient.

Conversions (non-linear — must use inverse relationships):

  • 30 MPG (US) = 7.84 L/100km = 12.75 km/L
  • 40 km/L = 2.5 L/100km = 94 MPG (US)

Digital Storage

  • 1 KB = 1,024 bytes (binary) or 1,000 bytes (SI)
  • 1 MB = 1,024 KB = 1,048,576 bytes
  • 1 GB = 1,024 MB
  • 1 TB = 1,024 GB

Quick Reference — Common Conversions by Country

USA-Specific

  • Height: feet/inches (5'10" = 177.8 cm)
  • Weight: pounds (150 lbs = 68.04 kg)
  • Temperature: Fahrenheit
  • Distance: miles
  • Fuel: MPG (US gallons)
  • Speed: MPH

UK-Specific

  • Body weight: stones and pounds (11 stone 4 = 71.7 kg)
  • Road distance: miles
  • Beer: pints (1 UK pint = 568 ml, vs 473 ml US pint)
  • Temperature: Celsius (officially) but Fahrenheit is still used colloquially

India-Specific

  • Gold: tola, carat
  • Land: bigha, gaj, cent, gunta, marla
  • Weight: maund, seer (old markets)
  • Fuel: km/L

Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries still use the imperial system?

The USA, Myanmar, and Liberia are the only countries that officially use the imperial system. The UK officially uses metric but retains imperial for roads (miles), body weight (stones), and beer (pints).

How do I convert height from feet/inches to cm?

Total inches = (feet × 12) + inches. Then multiply by 2.54 for cm. Example: 5'10" = 70 inches = 177.8 cm.
