What Is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method using timed intervals (typically 25 minutes of focused work, 5 minutes of rest).

Standard Pomodoro Cycle

  • Work Session: 25 minutes of focused work
  • Short Break: 5 minutes rest
  • Repeat: After 4 cycles, take a longer break (15-30 minutes)

Why Use Pomodoro?

  • Maintain focus and concentration
  • Break large tasks into manageable chunks
  • Prevent burnout with regular breaks
  • Track productivity metrics
  • Combat procrastination

Scientific Benefits

  • Improved focus: 25 minutes is the sweet spot for concentration
  • Reduced procrastination: Smaller time blocks feel less overwhelming
  • Better work-life balance: Regular breaks prevent burnout
  • Increased productivity: Studies show 25-30% productivity boost
