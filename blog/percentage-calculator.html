Real-Life Examples

Shopping Discount

Original Price: ₹2000, Discount: 30%
Discount Amount = ₹2000 × 30% = ₹600
Final Price = ₹2000 - ₹600 = ₹1400

Salary Increase

Current Salary: ₹50,000, Raise: 15%
Increase = ₹50,000 × 15% = ₹7,500
New Salary = ₹50,000 + ₹7,500 = ₹57,500

Restaurant Bill Tip

Bill Amount: ₹1200, Tip: 18%
Tip Amount = ₹1200 × 18% = ₹216
Total with Tip = ₹1200 + ₹216 = ₹1416
