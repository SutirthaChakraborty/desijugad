What Is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps determine if you're in a healthy weight range.

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category Health Status
Below 18.5 Underweight ⚠️ Nutritional risk
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight ✅ Healthy
25 - 29.9 Overweight ⚠️ Health risk
30+ Obese ⚠️ High health risk

How to Calculate BMI

Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m)²

Example: For 70 kg person, 175 cm tall
BMI = 70 / (1.75)² = 70 / 3.06 = 22.8 (Normal weight)
