Why Encode URLs?

URL encoding converts special characters and spaces into a format safe for URLs. It's essential for:

  • Creating shareable links with special characters
  • Handling query parameters safely
  • Debugging URL issues
  • API development and testing
  • Email and form submissions

Example: URL Encoding

Original: Hello World & Friends!
Encoded: Hello%20World%20%26%20Friends%21
