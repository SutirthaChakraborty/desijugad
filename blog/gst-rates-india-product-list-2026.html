GST Rates in India 2026 — Complete Product & Service List

Published April 28, 2026 · 10 min read · Updated for latest GST Council revisions

GST (Goods and Services Tax) unified India's fragmented tax structure in 2017. But with multiple slabs, exemptions, and regular GST Council revisions, figuring out "what GST rate applies to my product?" is still confusing. This guide covers 200+ items across all GST slabs with a free GST calculator to do the math instantly.

GST Slabs at a Glance

  • 0% (Nil GST): Essential goods and services — fresh food, health services, education
  • 5%: Basic necessities — household items, economy-class transport
  • 12%: Processed foods, business class travel, some construction materials
  • 18%: Standard rate — most goods and services, electronics, restaurants (AC)
  • 28%: Luxury and demerit goods — cars, tobacco, aerated drinks, casinos
  • Special rates: 3% (gold/silver), 1.5% (cut diamonds), 0.25% (rough diamonds)

0% GST — Nil Rate Items

Food & Agriculture

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables (unbranded)
  • Fresh/chilled meat (unbranded), fish, eggs
  • Milk, curd, lassi (unbranded, unpacked)
  • Unbranded flour (atta, maida, besan), rice, wheat
  • Salt, unprocessed honey
  • Fresh bread (except pizza bread)
  • Fresh ginger, garlic, onion (fresh/dried)
  • Tea leaves (unprocessed), raw coffee beans

Services

  • Healthcare services by hospitals and doctors
  • Education services by recognised institutions
  • Religious services
  • Agricultural services
  • Public transport (metro, local trains)

5% GST Items

Food & Beverages

  • Branded/packaged flour, rice, wheat
  • Edible oils (refined/unrefined)
  • Tea (packaged), coffee (processed)
  • Spices and condiments (packaged)
  • Sugar, jaggery
  • Packaged dried fruits (raisins, dates — basic)
  • Fish and fish products (processed)
  • Restaurants (non-AC, without liquor licence)

Other Items

  • Ayurvedic, Unani, Siddha medicines
  • Coal, coke, lignite
  • Fertilizers, pesticides
  • Economy class air travel
  • Handloom and coir products
  • Footwear below ₹1,000
  • Newspaper, magazines
  • Small residential under-construction flats (up to 45 sq mt)

12% GST Items

  • Processed food — butter, cheese, ghee, namkeen
  • Packed fruit juices, coconut water
  • Medicines (most pharma products)
  • Notebooks, exercise books, children's drawing books
  • Umbrellas
  • Sewing machines
  • Mobile chargers (some categories)
  • Business class air travel
  • Construction services for affordable housing
  • Agarbatti (incense sticks)
  • Apparel above ₹1,000
  • Furniture (some categories)

18% GST Items (Most Common)

Electronics & Technology

  • Mobile phones and smartphones
  • Laptops, computers, tablets
  • Televisions (up to 32 inches — 12%; above 32 — 18%)
  • Refrigerators, washing machines, ACs (below 2 ton)
  • Cameras, camcorders
  • Printers, scanners
  • IT services, software
  • Telecom services

Food & Beverages (18%)

  • Biscuits, chocolates, cocoa-based products
  • Ice cream, flavoured yoghurt
  • Fruit-based drinks, packaged juice concentrates
  • Restaurants (AC, with alcohol)
  • Pizza (except pizza bread)

Services (18%)

  • Banking and financial services
  • Insurance (general)
  • Outdoor catering
  • Event management
  • Consulting and professional services
  • Hotel rooms ₹1,000–₹7,500/night (12%); above ₹7,500 = 18%

Other 18% Items

  • Construction materials: cement, tiles, sanitary fittings
  • Paints, varnishes, putty
  • Safety glass, mirrors
  • Plastics and plastic products (most)
  • Stationery items
  • Detergents, soap (branded)
  • Footwear above ₹1,000

28% GST Items (Luxury & Sin Goods)

  • Cars, motorcycles, aircraft (personal use)
  • Aerated drinks and soft drinks
  • Tobacco products and cigarettes
  • Air conditioners above 2 ton
  • Large screen TVs (above 32 inches)
  • Dishwashers, vacuum cleaners
  • Paint (high-end), wallpaper
  • Firearms and ammunition
  • Casinos, race clubs, online gaming (GGR)
  • Luxury watches, sunglasses (premium)

GST on Services — Quick Reference

  • Restaurants: 5% (non-AC), 18% (AC with liquor licence)
  • Hotels: 12% (₹1,000–₹7,500/night), 18% (above ₹7,500)
  • Air travel: 5% (economy), 12% (business)
  • Banking fees: 18%
  • Insurance premiums: 18% (general), 18% (life insurance GST on risk component)
  • Freelance/consulting services: 18% (professional services)
  • Construction: 5% (affordable residential), 12% (other residential), 18% (commercial)
  • Transportation (road freight): 5% (with ITC), 12% (without ITC)

How to Calculate GST — Free Calculator

Use our free GST calculator for instant calculations. Here are the formulas:

Adding GST to a base price

GST Amount = Base Price × Rate / 100
Total = Base Price + GST Amount
Example: ₹10,000 product at 18% GST → GST = ₹1,800 → Total = ₹11,800

Removing GST from a GST-inclusive price

Base Price = Inclusive Price ÷ (1 + Rate/100)
Example: ₹11,800 inclusive at 18% → Base = ₹11,800 ÷ 1.18 = ₹10,000

CGST vs SGST vs IGST

  • CGST + SGST: For intra-state sales (within same state). Each takes half the GST rate. 18% GST = 9% CGST + 9% SGST.
  • IGST: For inter-state sales (across state borders). Full GST rate = IGST. 18% GST = 18% IGST.

GST Exemptions to Know

  • Healthcare: All medical services, medicines below exemption threshold
  • Education: Schools, colleges, coaching for competitive exams (partially exempt)
  • Agriculture: Seeds, fertilizers (5%), fresh produce (0%)
  • Small businesses: Turnover below ₹20 lakh/year (₹10 lakh for special category states) are exempt from GST registration
  • Composition scheme: Businesses below ₹1.5 crore can register under composition and pay 1-5% flat tax

For any specific product or service GST rate, check the official GST portal (gst.gov.in) or the CBIC notification for the latest updates.
