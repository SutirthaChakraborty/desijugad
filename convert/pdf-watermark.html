About PDF watermarking

Watermarks are text or graphics overlaid on document pages to indicate confidentiality (CONFIDENTIAL, DRAFT, DO NOT COPY), ownership, or status. This tool uses pdf-lib to embed the watermark natively in the PDF — not as a rendering layer.

FAQ

Is the original PDF modified?

No — a new PDF file is created in your browser with the watermark. Your original file remains unchanged.

Can the watermark be removed by recipients?

Because it is embedded at the PDF content level, removing it requires PDF editing tools. It is not trivially removable. For maximum protection combine with PDF password protection.
