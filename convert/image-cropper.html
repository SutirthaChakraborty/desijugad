FAQ

What is the passport photo size?

Standard Indian passport photos are 35×45 mm (35mm wide, 45mm tall), giving an aspect ratio of ~0.778. Select "Passport (35×45)" for this ratio.

Does cropping reduce quality?

The crop itself doesn't reduce quality. Choosing JPEG output applies JPEG compression — choose PNG for lossless output.
