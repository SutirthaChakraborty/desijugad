Why convert WebP or AVIF to JPG?

WebP and AVIF are modern formats offering better compression, but older apps, Windows Photo Viewer, and some social media platforms still need JPG/PNG. This tool uses your browser's Canvas API to decode any image format the browser supports and re-encodes it.

FAQ

Are my images uploaded?

No — conversion happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. No file leaves your device.

Why does quality only affect JPG/WebP and not PNG?

PNG is lossless — quality doesn't apply. JPG and WebP support lossy compression controlled by the quality setting.
