Why convert to WebP?

WebP is a modern image format by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression. WebP images are typically 25–35% smaller than JPEG at equivalent quality, and 25–34% smaller than PNG. This directly improves page load times and Core Web Vitals scores.

  • All modern browsers support WebP (Chrome, Firefox, Safari 14+, Edge).
  • WebP supports transparency (alpha channel) like PNG.
  • WebP supports animation like GIF.

FAQ

Will quality degrade visibly?

At quality 80–90, WebP is visually indistinguishable from JPEG. The default quality of 85 is a good balance for web use.

Are files uploaded?

No — conversion uses the browser Canvas API. Nothing leaves your device.
