Why convert GIF to video?

GIFs are an outdated format from 1987. Modern video formats (MP4/WebM) achieve the same looping animation at a fraction of the file size, with millions of colors instead of 256, and smoother playback. Almost all browsers support autoplay video as a GIF replacement.

FAQ

Will the video loop like a GIF?

On websites, yes — add loop attribute to the video element. On social media and messengers, looping behavior depends on the platform.

Does this work with static (non-animated) GIFs?

Yes, though converting a static GIF to video is rarely useful — the result is a very short single-frame video.
