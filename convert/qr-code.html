Generate QR codes for anything

QR codes encode text in a machine-readable 2D barcode. Smartphones can scan them with the built-in camera app — no extra app needed on Android or iOS.

Supported types

  • URL — Opens a web page when scanned.
  • Text — Displays any plain text message.
  • Email — Opens the mail app with pre-filled recipient and subject.
  • Phone — Dials a number when scanned.
  • SMS — Opens SMS to a number with optional prefilled message.
  • Wi-Fi — Connects to a Wi-Fi network automatically on Android/iOS.
  • vCard — Saves a contact to the phone's address book.

PNG vs SVG download

PNG is best for presentations, documents, and social media. SVG is a vector format — infinitely scalable, ideal for print materials and large signage.
