How it works

This tool uses the pdf-lib JavaScript library to read your PDF, apply the rotation to the selected pages, and generate a new PDF — entirely in your browser. Your original file is never modified.

FAQ

Will text and images rotate correctly?

Yes — pdf-lib rotates the page's viewport, so all content (text, images, vectors) rotates together.

Is there a page limit?

There's no hard limit, but very large PDFs (100+ pages, high-res images) may take a few seconds to process.
