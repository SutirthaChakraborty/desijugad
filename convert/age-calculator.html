Age calculator, down to the minute.

Enter your date of birth and find your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and even minutes. Also shows days until your next birthday.

How age is calculated

Age is calculated using the Gregorian calendar. The "years" value is the number of complete birthday anniversaries that have passed. Months and days are the remainder after accounting for full years.

FAQ

What is "Calculate Age As On"?

By default, age is calculated as of today. You can change the "As On" date to calculate age at a specific past or future date — useful for checking age eligibility on a particular date.

How are hours and minutes calculated?

The total difference is computed in milliseconds and divided down. The time assumes midnight (00:00) as the birth time since no birth time is entered.
