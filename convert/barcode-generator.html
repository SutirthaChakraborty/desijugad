Barcode format guide

  • CODE128 — Most versatile. Encodes all ASCII characters. Used in logistics, shipping labels.
  • EAN-13 — Standard retail barcode (13 digits). Used on products sold in stores.
  • UPC-A — 12-digit retail barcode used predominantly in North America.
  • CODE39 — Alphanumeric. Used in inventory, automotive, and defence.
  • ITF-14 — 14-digit barcode for outer packaging/cartons (GS1 standard).

FAQ

Is this generated in my browser?

Yes — JsBarcode runs entirely client-side. Nothing is uploaded.

Can I use these for products?

Yes, but retail EAN/UPC barcodes require a registered GS1 company prefix to be scannable in stores. For internal/inventory use, any value works.
