How to use the diff checker

Paste your original text in the left box and the modified version in the right box. Click Compare to see a line-by-line diff. Green lines are additions, red lines are deletions, unchanged lines are shown in white.

FAQ

Is my text sent to a server?

No. The diff algorithm runs entirely in JavaScript in your browser. Your text is never sent anywhere. This is fully private.

What is a "unified diff"?

A unified diff shows all lines in a single column — additions (+) and deletions (−) mixed with context lines. This is the standard format used by Git.
