How to split a PDF

Upload your PDF and choose one of three split modes. All processing happens in your browser using pdf-lib — nothing is sent to any server.

Custom ranges

Enter page groups separated by commas: 1-3, 4-6, 7. Each group becomes its own PDF. Perfect for splitting reports, chapters, or sections.

Every N pages

Automatically divide the PDF into equal-size chunks. Useful for splitting scanned books or large documents into manageable batches.

Individual pages

Download each page as its own PDF, bundled into a single ZIP file. Great for extracting specific diagrams, certificates, or slides.

FAQ

Can I split a password-protected PDF?

No — encrypted PDFs must be unlocked first. Use our PDF Unlock tool to remove the password, then split.

Will the output PDFs keep bookmarks and annotations?

Page content, images, and annotations are preserved. Document-level bookmarks pointing across split boundaries may not carry over, but per-page annotations are kept.

Multiple ranges download separately or as a ZIP?

If you split into 2+ parts using custom ranges or every-N mode, all resulting PDFs are bundled in a single ZIP archive for easy download.

Is there a page limit?

No limit is imposed by Desi Jugad. Very large PDFs (500+ pages) may take a few seconds to process in the browser — just be patient.
