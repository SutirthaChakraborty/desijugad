Regex flag reference

  • g — Global: find all matches, not just the first
  • i — Case insensitive: match regardless of uppercase/lowercase
  • m — Multiline: ^ and $ match start/end of each line
  • s — Dot-all: . matches newline characters too

FAQ

What regex syntax is supported?

JavaScript's built-in RegExp engine, which supports most common regex features: character classes, quantifiers, groups, lookahead, lookbehind, named groups, and Unicode categories.

How do I use capture groups?

Wrap part of your pattern in parentheses: (\d+)-(\w+). Each matched group is shown in the match details below.
