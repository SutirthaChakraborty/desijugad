FAQ

Why does camera scanning work better on mobile?

Mobile devices have rear cameras optimized for scanning. On desktop, ensure the barcode is well-lit and fills as much of the frame as possible.

Are images uploaded?

No — all processing happens in your browser using ZXing-js.
