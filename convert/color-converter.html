Color format guide

  • HEX — 6-digit hexadecimal (web standard). Example: #4F3CC9
  • RGB — Red, Green, Blue components (0–255). Example: rgb(79, 60, 201)
  • HSL — Hue (0–360°), Saturation (0–100%), Lightness (0–100%). Most intuitive for designers.
  • HSV/HSB — Hue, Saturation, Value. Used in many image editors (Photoshop, Figma).
  • CMYK — Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black. Used in print production.
