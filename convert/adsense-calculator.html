How AdSense earnings are calculated

AdSense revenue = Pageviews × CTR × CPC. CTR (Click-Through Rate) is the percentage of visitors who click an ad. CPC (Cost Per Click) is the amount earned per click. RPM (Revenue Per Mille) = CPC × CTR × 10.

FAQ

How accurate are these estimates?

Estimates are ballpark figures based on typical industry benchmarks. Actual earnings vary widely by audience geography, ad placement, content quality, and niche.

When does AdSense pay?

Google AdSense pays monthly when your balance reaches $100. Payments are issued between the 21st–26th of each month.
