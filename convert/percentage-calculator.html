  • X is what % of Y = (X ÷ Y) × 100
  • % change from X to Y = ((Y − X) ÷ X) × 100
  • Discount price = Original × (1 − Discount% ÷ 100)
  • Original price = Discounted ÷ (1 − Discount% ÷ 100)
