How to organize PDF pages

Upload your PDF and each page appears as a numbered card. Drag any card to a new position to reorder it. Click the trash icon to mark a page for deletion — it turns red. Click Restore deleted to undelete all. When you're happy with the order, click Save PDF to download the result.

Use cases

Remove blank pages from a scanned document. Put a title page at the front. Move an appendix to the end. Rearrange slide order in a presentation PDF. Delete duplicate or unwanted pages.

FAQ

Why do the page thumbnails show numbers instead of preview images?

Rendering actual PDF page previews requires PDF.js, a larger library. For speed and privacy, we use pdf-lib which reads pages without rendering. The page number clearly identifies each card, and dragging is just as intuitive.

Can I undo a delete?

Yes — deleted pages are shown in red but not removed until you click Save PDF. Click "Restore deleted" to unmark all deletions.

Does reordering preserve annotations and form fields?

Yes. pdf-lib copies full page content including annotations. Form fields on individual pages are preserved in the output.
