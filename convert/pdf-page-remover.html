How PDF page removal works

Upload your PDF, tell us which pages to delete, and we rebuild the document without them. All processing is client-side using pdf-lib — nothing is sent to a server.

Common use cases

Remove blank pages from scanned documents. Delete cover pages or watermark pages from downloaded PDFs. Remove duplicated pages. Clean up unwanted appendices or boilerplate.

Page range syntax

Enter a comma-separated list: 2,4,6-8 means remove pages 2, 4, 6, 7, and 8. The tool shows a live preview of how many pages will be removed versus kept.

FAQ

Can I remove the last page of a PDF?

Yes. Enter the last page number (shown in the file info line). For a 10-page PDF, enter 10.

What if I want to keep only a few pages instead of remove most?

Use our Extract Pages tool instead — specify the pages to keep rather than the pages to delete.

Will removing pages affect the original file?

No. The original file is never modified. A new PDF is generated and downloaded. Your original stays intact.
