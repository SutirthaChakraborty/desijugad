Turn a pile of photos into a single PDF

Whether you're scanning receipts with your phone, sending a batch of ID documents, or assembling a portfolio, bundling images into one PDF makes them easier to email, file, and print. This tool does it locally, without ever uploading your images.

Options that actually matter

  • Page size — standard A4 for most of the world, Letter for the US. Or let each image define its own page.
  • Orientation — choose portrait, landscape, or let the tool auto-pick based on each image.
  • Margin — whitespace around each image. Set to 0 for edge-to-edge.

FAQ

Can I reorder the images?

Yes — after adding files, use the drag handle on each item to reorder. The PDF will be generated in that order.

Is there a file limit?

No hard limit. Large batches (50+ high-res photos) may take 10–30 seconds to generate the PDF, depending on your device.

Will the PDF be searchable?

No — because images don't contain selectable text. If you need OCR/searchable PDFs, you'll want a different tool. We believe in single-purpose tools that do one thing well.
