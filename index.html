Why Desi Jugad

A converter that respects you.

01 / Privacy

Your files never leave your device.

Every conversion happens inside your browser using WebAssembly and JavaScript. We have no servers handling your data. Nothing to hack, nothing to leak, nothing to subpoena.

02 / Speed

Faster than uploading.

Because there's no round-trip to a server, your file is converted as fast as your computer can process it. On a modern laptop, that's usually a fraction of a second.

03 / Forever free

No plans. No upgrades. No limits.

There is no "pro" tier. No file size limits tied to a credit card. We keep the site running with unobtrusive ads and the occasional coffee from generous users.

04 / No account

You'll never see a sign-up screen.

We don't want your email. We don't want your name. Open the tool, use it, close the tab. That's the whole flow.

05 / Open formats

Built on web standards.

We use only open-source, peer-reviewed libraries — the same ones trusted by Google, Mozilla, and thousands of production apps. No proprietary black boxes.

06 / Accessible

Works on anything with a browser.

Windows, Mac, Linux, ChromeOS, iPad, Android phone — if it runs a modern browser, Desi Jugad works. No install, no app store review.

Dead simple

Pick a converter

Browse the library above or search for the format you need. Every tool has its own dedicated page.
