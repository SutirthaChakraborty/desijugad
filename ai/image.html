Generate AI images at cost price — no subscription, no margin

Most AI image tools charge a monthly subscription and limit how many images you can generate. Desi Jugad Image Studio does the opposite: you connect your own Replicate API key and every generation is billed directly to your account at Replicate's published rate. We don't add a single paisa of markup.

FLUX Schnell — the fastest model — costs $0.003 per image (about ₹0.25). FLUX 1.1 Pro costs $0.04 per image. At those prices, you can generate 1,000 images for $3–40 depending on the model. No subscription needed, no usage cap, no watermarks.

Which model should I choose?

FLUX Schnell is the right choice for quick experiments — it generates in about 5 seconds and is the cheapest option. FLUX 1.1 Pro is the workhorse for quality: photorealistic, detailed, and consistent. Ideogram v3 is unbeatable when your image needs readable text (signs, logos, posters). Recraft v3 SVG is the only model that outputs actual vector SVG files — perfect for logos, icons, and illustrations. Seedream 4.5 supports both generation and editing (describe what to change in an existing image).

Tips for better prompts

Be specific about lighting, mood, and camera angle. "A portrait" generates something generic; "a three-quarter portrait of a 30-year-old Punjabi farmer, golden hour backlight, shallow depth of field, shot on 85mm, film grain" generates something specific. Include the desired art style (photorealistic, watercolor, oil painting, vector illustration). Mention colors and composition. For FLUX, you don't need to add "high quality" — it's implied. For Ideogram, always include the exact text you want rendered.

Aspect ratios explained

1:1 is best for social profile pictures and Instagram posts. 16:9 is ideal for desktop wallpapers, thumbnails, and presentations. 9:16 is the vertical mobile/Stories format. 21:9 creates ultra-wide cinematic images suitable for YouTube banners or website headers.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?

Nothing. This runs entirely in your browser. Click Generate, and the request goes from your browser → our CORS proxy → Replicate → back. No installs, no desktop app.

How long does it take to generate an image?

FLUX Schnell is typically 3–8 seconds. FLUX 1.1 Pro and Ideogram v3 are 15–30 seconds. Recraft SVG can take up to 45 seconds. Replicate uses "warm" and "cold" starts — your first request of the day might take a bit longer.

Can I generate multiple images at once?

Yes — use the "Outputs" selector to generate 1, 2, or 4 images per request. Cost scales proportionally (4 images = 4× cost).

What is a negative prompt?

Words describing what you don't want in the image. Common negative prompts: "blurry, watermark, text overlay, low quality, distorted faces, extra limbs". Not all models support negative prompts equally.

Can I use the Remix button?

The Remix button on each generated image pre-fills the prompt with that image's seed number, so generating again produces a variation of that specific image instead of starting fresh.

Who owns the generated images?

Replicate's terms apply. Generally, images generated with open models like FLUX or Stable Diffusion are yours to use commercially. Always check the specific model's license on Replicate before commercial use.
