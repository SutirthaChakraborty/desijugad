Social Post Maker

Pick a platform, describe the post, download a correctly sized graphic.

 

Preview

Pick a platform, describe the post, hit Generate.

Pre-sized social graphics, generated free

Pick a platform — Instagram square, Story 9:16, LinkedIn 1.91:1, etc. — and the tool will generate an image at the correct dimensions. No more "image too small" warnings on Instagram. No more 16:9 thumbnails getting cropped on YouTube.

Default provider is Pollinations.ai: free, no key, fast. For richer typography and better text-in-image, switch to Gemini and add your free key in Settings.

Tips

  • Mention the colour palette explicitly ("saffron and indigo, on cream").
  • Mention the mood ("festive", "minimal", "techy").
  • For text rendering, Gemini handles short headlines better than Pollinations. Keep text under 8 words for best results.
  • For Stories, leave 200 px of negative space at top and bottom for the platform UI.
