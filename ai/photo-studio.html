AI-powered photo tools — professional results from your phone camera

Getting a good passport photo in India used to mean finding a photo studio, paying ₹50–100, and hoping the framing meets the specifications. A professional LinkedIn headshot could cost ₹3,000–10,000 at a photography studio. Desi Jugad AI Photo Studio does both — and much more — for fractions of a rupee per image, using AI models running on Replicate's infrastructure.

Passport photo specifications

The passport photo template automatically formats your photo to standard specifications: 35mm × 45mm at 600 DPI, white background, face occupying 70–80% of the frame. The AI removes the original background, adjusts lighting to ensure even illumination with no harsh shadows, and centres the face with the correct margins. Suitable for Indian passport, Aadhaar update, visa applications, and most government document photo requirements.

Important: for official documents, always verify the final image against the specific document's requirements. AI-generated photos are highly accurate but you are responsible for final compliance.

LinkedIn profile photo

The LinkedIn headshot template transforms a casual selfie into a professional headshot with a clean background, adjusted lighting, and a polished look that works on professional networks. The AI maintains your likeness while improving exposure, removing distracting backgrounds, and applying a subtle professional portrait style.

Photo restoration

The restoration template uses face restoration models (GFPGAN and CodeFormer available on Replicate) to repair old, damaged, or low-quality photographs. It recovers detail in faces, fixes color fading, and removes scratches and noise — without adding fake details that weren't there. Ideal for old family photographs scanned from prints.

Lighting and cinematic grade

The "Better lighting" template re-illuminates your photo with professional studio lighting — Rembrandt, butterfly, or split lighting patterns. The "Cinematic" template applies a film-grade color treatment: raised blacks, warm highlights, subtle grain, and a widescreen crop if your photo is portrait orientation. Both templates use FLUX-based inpainting models on Replicate.

Models used

Photo editing templates use these Replicate models depending on the task: FLUX 1.1 Pro for background replacement and style transfer; Clarity Upscaler for upscaling and detail recovery; GFPGAN and CodeFormer for face restoration; Recraft v3 for passport-style background replacement. You can select Gemini's Imagen 3 as an alternative provider in settings for image editing tasks.

Privacy

Your photo is sent to Replicate (via our CORS proxy) as a base64-encoded image in the API request. Replicate's data retention policy applies. Desi Jugad does not store your photo. Your Replicate API key is stored only in your browser.

Frequently asked questions

Will my passport photo be accepted by government offices?

The AI photo meets standard dimensional and background specifications for most Indian government documents. However, different offices can be strict about specifics. Always print and check before submitting. If rejected, you can regenerate with different settings or use a local photo studio for the final print.

What kind of source photo works best?

A well-lit selfie or a photo where your face is clearly visible works best. For passport photos: neutral expression, facing directly at the camera, no sunglasses, no heavy shadows. For LinkedIn headshots: any reasonably clear photo works — the AI will handle the background and lighting.

How much does it cost per photo?

Typically $0.04–$0.08 per generation (₹3–7). Passport photo mode generates 4 copies at once (for a print sheet), so expect around $0.06 total. Restoration can vary from $0.03 to $0.10 depending on image size.

Can I use a Gemini API key instead of Replicate?

Yes. Add your Gemini API key in AI Settings and select "Gemini" as the provider. Imagen 3 (Google's image editing model) supports background removal and style transfer. Some templates are Replicate-only; the tool will indicate which provider is required.

My photo came out wrong — what can I do?

Use the custom prompt override to add specific instructions: "keep the glasses", "brighter background", "more natural skin tone". You can also generate multiple variations by clicking Generate again — each run uses a different seed.

Can I restore a very old, black-and-white photo?

Yes. Use the "Photo Restoration" template. It handles B&W photos, adds colorisation if you check that option, and repairs damage. Results are best when the face is relatively large in the frame. Very heavily damaged photos may need the "Clarity Upscale" template as a first pass.
