Privacy

All keys are stored under dj_ai_keys_v1 in your browser's localStorage. They are sent only to the relevant provider (Google or Hugging Face) — never to desijugad.co.in. Clear your browser data and they're gone.

FAQ

Why does Gemini work in the browser but Replicate / OpenAI don't?

Browser security (CORS) lets a website call only those APIs that explicitly allow cross-origin requests. Google Gemini sets that header — so we can call it directly from your browser with your key. Replicate, OpenAI, and Anthropic don't, so they require a server-side proxy. We chose to remove the proxy and stay 100% static, which means we keep only the providers that work browser-direct.

Where do I get a Gemini key?

Visit aistudio.google.com/apikey, sign in with any Google account, and click "Create API key". Free tier gives you a generous monthly quota.

Is the free Pollinations option safe?

Pollinations is an open project that returns generated images directly as image URLs. There's no key, no sign-up, and no data sent except the prompt itself. If their servers are slow, the tool tells you and offers a retry.
