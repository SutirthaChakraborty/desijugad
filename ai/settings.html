Privacy Confirmation
Your keys are stored in this browser only.

Keys live in localStorage under dj_ai_keys_v1. They are never sent to any Desi Jugad server. Our CORS proxy only passes them along to Replicate or Suno verbatim — it does not log them, store them, or read them.

Close this tab and keys stay local. Clear browser data and they're gone permanently. You can also click "Clear" above at any time.

Important: Storing API keys in localStorage is convenient but not airtight. A malicious browser extension or XSS flaw could read them. For high-value enterprise tokens, consider running Desi Jugad AI Studio locally by cloning the GitHub repo.
