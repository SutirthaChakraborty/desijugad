The most private background remover online

Most "free background remover" sites upload your image to their server, run the model there, and send you the result. Some keep your image. Some train models on it. Desi Jugad's remover does none of that — the model runs in your browser using WebAssembly + WebGPU. Once loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and it still works.

How it works

We use the open-source @imgly/background-removal library, which loads a small ONNX model (~30 MB) and runs inference locally. The model is fetched from jsDelivr CDN on first use and cached by the browser, so subsequent runs are instant.

Quality vs. speed

  • Works best on clear subjects (people, products) against contrasting backgrounds.
  • Hair detail and fine edges are good — competitive with paid services.
  • Performance: a few seconds per image on a modern laptop with WebGPU; up to 15–30 s on older devices using WASM fallback.
  • If your browser doesn't support WebGPU (Safari < 17), it falls back to WASM. Slower, same quality.
