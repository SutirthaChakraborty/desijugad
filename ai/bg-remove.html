How this works

This tool uses @imgly/background-removal, an open-source library (MIT license) that ships an ONNX AI segmentation model. The model runs entirely inside your browser via WebAssembly — no data ever leaves your device.

The first time you use it, your browser downloads the model files (~5 MB). These are cached in your browser's storage and reused on subsequent visits — so the second run is instant.

What it works best on

  • Portraits — faces, shoulders, full-body shots with clear subject separation.
  • Product photos — items on simple or single-colour backgrounds.
  • Animals — works well on pets with distinct fur colours.
  • Logos & graphics — flat objects on clean backgrounds.

Limitations

  • Complex scenes with many overlapping objects may have rough edges.
  • Very high-resolution images (10 MP+) may be slow on low-end devices.
  • Glass, water, and transparent objects are notoriously difficult for all AI removers.

FAQ

Is this really free with no API key?

Yes. The AI model runs in WASM inside your browser. Unlike tools that call cloud APIs (Replicate, Remove.bg), this requires no API key and incurs no cost per image.

How does quality compare to paid tools?

For common subjects (people, products) quality is very close to Remove.bg and Canva's background remover. For complex hair or transparent subjects, cloud tools with larger models may do better.
