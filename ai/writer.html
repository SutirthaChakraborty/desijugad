An AI writer that doesn't sell your data

This writer talks to Google's Gemini API directly from your browser using your own key. We never see your prompts, your output, or your key. The free tier is generous enough for casual blog writing — most users never hit the limit.

What works well

  • Indian-context blog posts and how-tos (Gemini knows local nuance — UPI, GST, RBI, Aadhaar — better than most US-trained models).
  • LinkedIn updates that don't sound like AI slop.
  • Hinglish drafts, English drafts, transliterated Hindi.
  • Outlines for long-form content before you commit to writing.

What doesn't

  • Anything requiring real-time information (current stock prices, today's news) — Gemini's training cutoff applies.
  • Heavily formatted exports — copy-paste into your CMS and reformat there.
