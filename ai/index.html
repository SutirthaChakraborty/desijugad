Why bring your own key?

Fair pricing by design.

01 / Privacy

Your keys, your data, your generations.

API keys live in your browser's localStorage — not on any Desi Jugad server. Your prompts go straight from your browser to Replicate or Suno via a dumb CORS proxy that never logs anything. Close the tab and it's gone.

02 / Cost transparency

Replicate's published rate. Nothing extra.

FLUX Schnell costs $0.003 per image. Veo 3.1 costs $0.50 per second. You see the estimate before you click Generate. You pay Replicate directly. Desi Jugad takes exactly zero rupees of margin.

03 / Model choice

20+ models. We add new ones weekly.

When Google ships Veo 4 or Suno launches V6, we add it the same week. You're not locked into whatever model a SaaS vendor decided is "premium". Pick the right tool for the job.

AI generation tools that don't nickel-and-dime you

Most AI tools charge you a subscription and take a fat margin on every generation. You pay $30/month and get access to one version of one model with usage caps. Desi Jugad AI Studio works differently: you bring your own Replicate API key and pay the model provider at their published rate, with zero markup from us.

What is the BYOK model?

BYOK stands for Bring Your Own Key. Instead of Desi Jugad paying Replicate and charging you more, you connect your own account directly. When you generate an image, the request goes: your browser → our CORS proxy (which reads nothing) → Replicate → back to your browser. Your images, videos, and music never touch a Desi Jugad server.

Which models are available?

For images: FLUX 1.1 Pro, FLUX Schnell, FLUX 2 Pro, Ideogram v3, Recraft v3 SVG, Seedream 4.5, and more. For video: Veo 3.1, Kling 3.0, Seedance 2.0, Wan 2.7. For music: Suno V5.5 and Lyria 3 Pro. For writing: Claude Opus 4.7, Llama 3.3 70B, Mistral Large. We track new releases on Replicate's official collection and add them within days.

Is Replicate expensive?

Not at all. FLUX Schnell (fast, good quality) costs $0.003 per image — that's ₹0.25 per image. You can generate 100 images for $0.30. FLUX 1.1 Pro (best quality) is $0.04 per image. Video is more expensive (~$0.25/sec for Veo Fast), which is why we show cost estimates before you generate. Music via Suno is around $0.02 per song on most plans.

What about privacy?

Your API keys are stored in localStorage under the key dj_ai_keys_v1. They are never sent to desijugad.co.in — only to the relevant model provider API (Replicate or Suno) via the CORS proxy, which is a dumb pass-through. The proxy code is open source and you can audit it. Clear your browser data and the keys are gone. No account required.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to sign up for Desi Jugad?

No account, no email, no sign-up. Same as our converters. Just paste your Replicate or Suno key in settings and start generating.

Where do I get a Replicate API key?

Sign up at replicate.com, go to Account → API tokens, and create a token. Add a card to your account — you only pay for what you generate. New accounts often get some free credit to start.

What is Suno, and why is it a "wrapper"?

Suno makes an AI music generation product. As of 2026, they don't publish an official public API, so third-party services like sunoapi.org provide API access to their models. You get a key from sunoapi.org (or kie.ai, piapi.ai) and paste it in our Suno settings. The API shape is nearly identical across wrappers, so our code works with any of them.

Can I use a Gemini API key for photos?

Yes. The Photo Studio supports Replicate models (FLUX, face restoration, background removal) and you can also enter a Gemini key in settings for models like Imagen 3. Select "Gemini" as the provider on the photo studio page.

What happens if I run out of Replicate credits?

The API returns a 402 error and we show a clear message: "You're out of credits" with a direct link to top up your Replicate account. Your key stays saved; just add more credits and continue.

Are generations stored anywhere?

Replicate stores your generations on their servers for a limited time (they have their own retention policy). Desi Jugad does not store anything. Your usage log is local to your browser.
